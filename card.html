Radhe-Radhe

The protagonist of the film "RADHE" is a smart and tough cop with a kind heart and witty personality. He usually likes to do things his way to get justice restored in society. How far RADHE goes to bring justice, clean the streets from drugs to save the youth and to restore peace in society forms the crux of the story. The story revolves around the rampant usage of drugs in our society and how the youth is getting destroyed in the bargain.
